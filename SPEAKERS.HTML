Speakers

Steven Soderbergh

VISIONARY OF THE MODERN DAY CINEMA

When a film is made today, there are big differences between the look and feel of the films when compared to older films, for example, films from Directors like Alfred Hitchcock. Modern cinematography has completely changed the way films are viewed, as we’d expect. It’s often obvious what the difference is, the industry has moved on, the budgets are higher and the technology is more advanced.

Steven is going to take us on a long journey of the creation of the modern day cinema which we witness today.

About Steven

Steven is a commercial director and has a shot over a 100s of shorts and more than 20 movies.

Douglas Sirk

SCREENPLAYS ARE EVERYTHING

The screenplay is the guide for the Producers, Director, Actors, Crew of exactly what will appear on screen. It is the common ground that everybody on the film will work with from start to finish of production. It tells the complete story, contains all action in the film and all dialogue for each character. It can also describe characters visually so filmmakers can try to capture their style, look or vibe.

About Douglas

Douglas is a very famous screenplay and has written over 50 screenplays in his life

Bob Robertson

POURING(WRITING) LIFE TO THE SCENE

Dialogue is the most important part of the film because it makes the film seem mature and solidly crafted. A movie with bland dialogue and cheesy lines comes off as immature and makes the screenwriter look like he didn’t try his hardest to create the best film he could

Dialogue is more than just another aspect of film. It can elivate a movie to a new level. Bob will be sharing his experience as a Dialogue Writer in the film industry

About Bob

Bob is a dialouge writer and has written for many blockbuster movies which are still relevants due to its impactful dialouges.

Akhilesh Ghosh

WHAT IT TAKES TO BE A PRODUCER

Being a producer is one of the most challenging jobs and also the most difficult to define. This possibly explains why most newcomers want to become directors and writers instead. Even at the most prestigious film schools in the world, there’s a lot more focus on courses based on directing, writing, acting and other artistic careers. But in recent times, there has been an increased appreciation for the role of a producer, more precisely a Creative Producer

About Akhilesh

Akhilesh is a well-known producer and has produced many hit films in his career
